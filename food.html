Tahu Aci


Tofu aci is a typical Brebes cuisine made from tofu, aci, and spices. Then fried until dry. The outside texture is crispy while the inside is soft and chewy. The fried aci tofu is perfect for snacking. This Aci Tofu can be easily found in a number of restaurants in Brebes.

Sate Blenggong

Sate blengong is one of the mandatory foods that we need to try when visiting Brebes. This satay is made from duck or duck meat and can be enjoyed with kupat (ketupat). We can choose two servings for this sate blengong, grilled like satay in general or boiled. Boiled sate blengong certainly has a different taste, boiled with coconut milk. We can also easily find this sate blengong seller around the Brebes square.
