Ranto Canyon

Those who are interested in having a challenging vacation in Brebes, Ranto Canyon can be a destination for tourists. Ranto Canyon Waterfall can be reached after following the 769 meter long river. Along the way, you will be presented with exotic views. The journey is even more fun when you pass through a river that is flanked by two cliffs.

Panen Joansalem

Located in Babakan Hamlet, Wanoja Village, Salem District, this hill is very exotic. From this hill, you can see the beauty of Brebes from above. As far as the eye can see, you are treated to lined hills and a stretch of green trees that cover the hills. If you want to look more instagenic, the manager also provides various properties such as bamboo tables and chairs and an old television. There is also a viewing post that you can use there. From downtown Brebes, you can reach this object in 3 hours or about 70 km. But don't worry, this hill can be reached either by motorbike or car.
