My Hometown, Brebes

This time, I will tell you about my hometown, Brebes. Brebes itself is located in the province of Central Java, Indonesia. Usually I go back to Brebes once a year, namely on Eid al-Fitr. However, since the covid-19 pandemic I haven't been there for a long time. The most famous thing in my hometown is usually the culinary ones, such as onions and salted eggs.
